The Platforming Genre

I have a lot of hobbies that I like to do during my free time but the one hobby that stayed with me since the beginning was playing video games. I've been playing them since I was probably 3 years old and played a lot of different genres of it. However the one genre that resonated with me early on was platformers. Examples of platformers include Super Mario Bros, Sonic The Hedgehog, Crash Bandicoot, Mega Man etc. Some can be 2D or 3D, with objectives that are simple such as beating a level, or more complex where you would have to navigate through a maze to get to the goal. The platforming genre was certainly a popular genre back in the day, and although they are not as common today, the audience for them is still out there. My personal favorite platformer is Super Mario Galaxy 2.
